Educational Purpose

This interactive guide helps identify common antisemitic rhetoric patterns. Click on each box to learn about these harmful narratives and how to recognize them.

While by no means complete, this project draws inspiration from the IHRA Working Definition of Antisemitism. We encourage readers to explore this vital subject further and develop their own understanding of how to recognize and counter hate speech in all its forms.

Remember: Education and awareness are our most powerful tools against hatred and discrimination.
